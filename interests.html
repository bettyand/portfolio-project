Flight Rising
Flight Rising is dragon-themed pet sim browser game I have been playing since 2017. When I was furloughed due to COVID in 2020/2021, I got involved in community-run events on the site to keep busy and make friends. The spreadsheet, bbcode, and organizational skills I learned in the process gave me the confidence to learn actual programming.
